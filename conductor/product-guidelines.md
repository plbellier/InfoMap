# Product Guidelines - InfoMap

## Prose and Voice
- **Tone:** Professional and Objective. The application should present news and data in a neutral, factual manner, suitable for students and educators. Summaries should be concise but informative.

## Design and Visual Identity
- **Navigation:** Smooth Pan & Zoom. The map should offer fluid movement with hover effects to highlight countries, ensuring a polished and modern interaction experience.
- **Color Palette:** Emerald Green and Deep Charcoal. This palette supports the futuristic dark mode while remaining sophisticated and comfortable for extended reading.

## Accessibility
- **Contrast:** High color contrast must be maintained for all text elements and map overlays to ensure readability across different lighting conditions and for users with visual impairments.
